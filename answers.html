Programming

Programming is the process of creating instructions that a computer can follow to perform specific tasks. It involves writing code in languages like Python, Java, or C++ to solve problems, automate processes, or build applications. At its core, programming combines logic, creativity, and problem-solving to bring ideas to life in the digital world. Whether building websites, games, or complex systems, programming is essential in shaping the technology that drives our daily lives.
